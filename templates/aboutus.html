About Us

We are the students of computer engineering trying to solve the global day-to-day problem by providing a platform or a solution for that particular problem. Here as the name suggests E-Plastic Management System is used for the collection of plastic waste material from the customer, local collector, so on. The customer can define the details about which type of plastic waste is having. The customer can get the money by giving away the plastic waste. E-plastic Management System is for selling and buying the plastic waste material. The main purpose of the E-Plastic Management System is to provide a platform so that the human society can sell and buy the resuable and recyclable plastic materials. This will reduce the plastic waste from household, company, industries, city, etc. The Environment Pollution as well as plastic production will reduce. This technique could eliminate plastic disposal costs, reduce raw material costs and provide income from a salable plastic waste.

Our Vision

India to become a leading Circular Economy where Nothing is Waste.
